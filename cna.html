Contact & About

Primo is a community run media outlet aiming to curate and cover the best of the Session fakeskate scene. Our staff is comprised of lifelong skaters, some with industry experience, working to bring the feeling of real skateboarding media to fakeskate. In addition to this blog, we have released several full magazine issues (available in the Mag tab), host curated videos on our YouTube channel, and hold community events for those in our Discord server.
